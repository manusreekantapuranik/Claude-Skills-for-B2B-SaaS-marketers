# Messaging Framework 

## What
A complete messaging document that defines the value proposition, elevator pitch, long description, customer requirements, and outcome pillars for a B2B SaaS product and persona. 
Saved as: outputs/messaging-framework.docx 

## How 

### Step 1 - Load context 
Read icp.md 
Read brand.md
Read voice.md
Read outputs/jtbd-analysis.docx
Read outputs/positioning.docx 

### Step 2 - Define the value proposition 
Write a clear succinct statement of the unique value the product offers. 
Rules:
- 10 to 15 words maximum
- One clear focal point
- Tied to positioning
- No jargon
- Speaks directly to the ICP

### Step 3 - Write the audience profile 
Brief background on the persona. 
Cover: 
- Title and role 
- Key responsibilities
- Personality
- Role in the buying process

3 to 5 sentences maximum.

### Step 4 - Write the elevator pitch 
1 to 2 sentences. Must include: 
- What makes this product unique
- Top one to two value points
- Emotional connection
- Reference to the target market 

### Step 5 - Write the long description 
100 to 200 words covering: 
- Core value points from positioning
- Product and feature details
- Headline benefits
- Target market indication
- Proof points if available 

### Step 6 - Define customer requirements 
Identify 1 to 2 things that are crucial to the persona to convert. These are the non-negotiables the product must address before the buyer will consider purchasing. 

### Step 7 - Build outcome pillars 
Define 3 outcome pillars that substantiate the elevator pitch and positioning. For each pillar define: 
- Pillar name (1 to 3 words)
- Pain point it solves
- Product benefits that address it
- Product details that create those benefits
- Proof point that validates it 

Rules for proof points: 
- Specific and real
- Include name, company, timeframe, and measurable result
- Different proof points for different persona pain points

### Step 8 - Review and align
Check the full document against:
- JTBD analysis output
- Positioning output
- ICP profile

Ensure the messaging:
- Reflects the functional job
- Connects to the emotional job
- Uses language the ICP uses
- Is free of jargon

### Step 9 - Produce output document 
Create a Word document with these sections in this exact order: 

[Product name] Messaging for [Persona name]

VALUE PROPOSITION
Single statement. 10 to 15 words. 

AUDIENCE
Brief persona profile. 3 to 5 sentences.

ELEVATOR PITCH
1 to 2 sentences. 

LONG DESCRIPTION
100 to 200 words. 

Save as: outputs/messaging-framework.docx
