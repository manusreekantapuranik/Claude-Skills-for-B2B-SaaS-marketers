# Launch Deck Skill

## Description
Creates comprehensive B2B SaaS product launch presentation decks with proven slide structure and messaging frameworks.

## When to trigger
- When asked for a launch deck
- When asked for a pitch deck  
- When asked for launch slides
- When asked for a GTM presentation
- Works standalone or as part of full agent workflow

## What this skill does
Builds a 9-slide product launch deck covering market problem, target audience, product overview, competitive landscape, marketing strategy, and launch timeline.

## Instructions

### Step 1 - Gather launch information
Ask the user for:
- Product name and one-line description
- Target audience and key persona
- Main problem the product solves
- Key product features and benefits
- Top 3 competitors
- Launch timeline and key milestones
- Success metrics

### Step 2 - Build the deck structure

**SLIDE 1 - COVER**
- Product name large and centered
- Tagline directly below
- Launch date or version below tagline
- Company branding elements
- Clean, minimal, confident design

**SLIDE 2 - EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**
- What is being launched
- Primary launch goal
- Key timeline milestones
- Top 3 success metrics
- One screen, no scrolling

**SLIDE 3 - MARKET PROBLEM**
- Lead with the pain the target audience lives with
- 3 to 5 specific pain points
- Quantify where possible
- End with the cost of doing nothing

**SLIDE 4 - TARGET AUDIENCE**
- Primary persona name and title
- Key responsibilities
- What they care most about
- What keeps them up at night
- Simple card layout

**SLIDE 5 - PRODUCT OVERVIEW**
- Product name and one line description
- How it works in 3 simple steps
- 5 key features with benefits
- Steps numbered clearly

**SLIDE 6 - COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE**
- Visual comparison matrix
- Rows are key buying criteria
- Columns are this product and top 3 competitors
- Strong, Partial, or Weak ratings
- Highlight your product's advantages

**SLIDE 7 - MARKETING STRATEGY**
- Pre-launch activities
- Launch day activities
- Post-launch activities
- Key channels and messages
- Three column layout or timeline

**SLIDE 8 - LAUNCH TIMELINE**
- Visual timeline from today to launch
- Key milestones marked clearly
- Phase distinctions (pre-launch, launch, post-launch)

**SLIDE 9 - THANK YOU**
- Large centered "Thank you"
- Product name below
- Primary CTA below that (contact, demo link, or next step)
- Brand accent element
- Confident close, not decorative

### Step 3 - Review and refine
Check every slide:
- Brand colors consistent throughout
- Company fonts used everywhere
- Brand accents on key elements
- Every slide has one clear message
- No slide is too text heavy
- Product name visible on every slide
- Narrative flows logically slide to slide

### Step 4 - Output format
Present the complete deck content organized by slide number with clear headers, bullet points, and suggested visual treatments.

## Example output format

**SLIDE 1: COVER**
[Product Name]
Tagline: [One compelling line about the product]
Launch: [Date/Quarter Year]
[Suggested: Brand accent line or minimal logo treatment]

**SLIDE 2: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**
What: [Product launch description]
Goal: [Primary success outcome]
Timeline: [Key dates]
Metrics: [3 success measures]

[Continue for all 9 slides...]
